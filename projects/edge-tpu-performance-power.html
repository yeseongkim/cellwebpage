Online performance and power prediction for edge TPU via comprehensive characterization

Comprehensive characterization turns Edge TPU behavior into predictive models for performance and power-aware deployment.

Authors Y. Ni, Y. Kim*, T. Rosing, and M. Imani*

Abstract

What the paper is doing.

In this paper, we characterize and model the performance and power consumption of Edge TPU, which efficiently accelerates deep learning (DL) inference in a low-power environment. Systolic array, as a high throughput computation architecture, its usage in the edge excites our interest in its performance and power pattern. We perform an extensive study for various neural network settings and sizes using more than 10,000 DL models. Through comprehensive exploration, we profile which factors highly influence the inference time and power to run DL Models. We show our key remarks for the relation between the performance/power and DL model complexity to enable hardware-aware optimization and design decisions.
