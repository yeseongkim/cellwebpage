Abstract

What the paper is doing.

Machine learning methods have been widely utilized to provide high quality for many cognitive tasks. Running sophisticated learning tasks requires high computational costs to process a large amount of learning data. Brain-inspired Hyperdimensional Computing (HDC) is introduced as an alternative solution for lightweight learning on edge devices. However, HDC models still rely on accelerators to ensure realtime and efficient learning. These hardware designs are not commercially available and need a relatively long period to synthesize and fabricate after deriving the new applications.
